Anika Zaman, for assisting with plasmid design and organising protein complex components, proofreading wet lab workflow and procedures, and providing critical insight on the feasibility of the technical aspects of the project.

Sebastian Alvarez, for connecting us to Amir Varamesh for HP insight, suggesting that we express the different components of the BC synthesis pathway separately in E. coli to identify bottlenecks and optimise expression, and providing critical insight on the feasibility of the technical aspects of the project.

Andrew Symes for providing lots of insight throughout the project development process to the drylab subprojects and overall project direction and assisting in any wiki difficulties and shortcomings.

Dewuni De Silva, for guiding the Wiki design and providing a sense of scope for the content to include, and providing general insight on the feasibility of our project.

Arshia Mostoufi, for providing critical insight on the feasibility of the technical aspects of the project, including the lysozyme expression and subsequent antibacterial solutions, as well as the E. coli expression pivot.

Rigel Tormon for providing guidance to the drylab subprojects, especially modelling.

Dr. Mayi Arcellana-Panlilio for supervising and encouraging the iGEM Calgary 2022 team.
