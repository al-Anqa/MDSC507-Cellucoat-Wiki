pET-16b Vector Description

Figure 1: pET-16b vector containing malE-lysozyme-IVY gene construct. Information for each vector adapted from “PET bacterial recombinant protein vector”.

  • T7 promoter - promotes high levels of transcription using a T7 polymerase
  • malE - signal peptide used to express lysozyme gene complex out of the periplasm space of the cell to minimize toxicity
  • Lysozyme - gene of interest
  • TEV protease - used to remove IVY and have purified lysozyme protein for immobilization
  • IVY - lysozyme inhibitor to minimize cell toxicity
  • Terminator - prevent run-on transcription
  • Amp - ampicillin antibiotic resistance
  • oriC - pBR322 origin of replication
  • ROP - Repressor of primer used to regulate plasmid copy number
  • Lac I - Lac repressor sequence for inducible expression of gene construct
